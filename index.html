Charging Infrastructure(Covers all Highways and bypass roads near Pune.)

Welcome to a future where electric mobility is not only sustainable but also convenient. At CIM, we are leading the charge in transforming city infrastructure with our state-of-the-art EV power stations. Strategically and conveniently located across the city near popular landmarks, you can relax and enjoy your time while your vehicle gets a surge, our charging network ensures that electric vehicle owners have easy access to fast and reliable charging. With cutting-edge technology, user-friendly interfaces, and a commitment to exceptional service, we are shaping the landscape of urban transportation.

Portable charging(Charging through Electric Vans.)

Now you can say goodbye to ‘Range-Anxiety’ and hello to the new in town Portable charging solution. Vans running on electric power, carrying a mini charging station will come to your aid, whenever, wherever. We are the first company to provide this service of portable charging through electric vans.

Harnessing the Power of Reuse.

At CIM, we're driving innovation by reimagining the potential of electric vehicle (EV) batteries. Instead of disposing of them, we repurpose these batteries, extending their lifespan and reducing waste. Our cutting-edge techniques unlock new applications in residential energy storage, grid stabilization, and more. By reusing EV batteries, we promote sustainability, empower communities, and create a greener future. Join us in embracing the power of repurposing to drive positive change.
